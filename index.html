World of Webcams

This site combines Google Maps and Windy's truly massive repository of webcams! (55,000+)
Many of the webcams are 24 hour+ timelapses, but there are thousands of live cameras too. Have fun!

How to Use

  • Pan to an area of the map or select "Find Me" to geolocate your position
  • The map will show cameras in the area and group nearby cameras
  • Zoom in further to see individual cams and click on the marker

Notes

  • When no filters are active, map markers will cluster into groups
  • If any filters are active, webcam clustering is disabled and only 50 markers can be shown (Windy API limitation)
  • You can see above the map if any camera markers are being hidden. To see them, zoom in further or filter by categories.

  • To see grouped map markers again, toggle filters off
