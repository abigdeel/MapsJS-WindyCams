How to Use

  • Panning or zooming refreshes the map and populates camera markers.
  • The map will show cameras in the area and group nearby cameras.
  • Tap the camera icon to see more info and webcam links. Tap the number above the marker to zoom in further.
  • Click anywhere on the map to close any open info windows.

Notes

  • When no filters are active, map markers will cluster into groups.
  • If any filters are active, webcam clustering is disabled and only 50 markers can be shown (Windy limitation)
  • You can see above the map how many markers are currently shown and if any are hidden. To see them, zoom in further or filter by categories.
  • Live streams will always open in a new window when you hit play.

  • To see grouped map markers again, toggle filters off
